The Photographer

S. Gajanan

Based in Bandarawela, I believe every moment carries a universe of emotion. My lens is not just a camera — it's a journal.

With over a decade behind the camera, I've had the privilege of documenting weddings, corporate events, school milestones, and intimate pre-shoots across Sri Lanka. My style blends natural light with cinematic composition to create images that feel alive years later.

Equipped with professional-grade DSLR systems and a DJI Mini 3 drone, I bring a full production capability to every assignment — from intimate portraits to large-scale real estate shoots.
